Tedashii - Dead Or Alive, Pt. 1

Dead or Alive Pt. 1, is the first of a series of EP's where Tedashii's vulnerably shares the various ways he has “fought for his life” and what he has learned in that process. “At several points in my own life, I've felt crushed under the weight of so many hardships and I've even attempted to end it all.
